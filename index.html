About the City

Solapur is a historic and culturally rich city located in the southern part of Maharashtra[cite: 3]. It is known for its spiritual centers, historic monuments, and vibrant culture[cite: 4]. For centuries, it has been an important destination for pilgrims and travelers[cite: 5].
