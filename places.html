Landmarks & Spiritual Centers

Siddheshwar Temple

Located near Siddheshwar Lake, this temple is dedicated to Lord Siddheshwar[cite: 16]. It is a major landmark, especially during the Siddheshwar Yatra in January[cite: 17].

Pandharpur

Situated 70 km from Solapur, Pandharpur is famous for the Vitthal Rukmini Temple[cite: 19, 20]. It attracts millions during the Wari pilgrimage[cite: 21].

Tuljapur Heritage

A significant pilgrimage site near Solapur, completing the spiritual circuit for travelers[cite: 11].
