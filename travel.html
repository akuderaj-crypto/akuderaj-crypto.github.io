Getting There

By Road [cite: 51]

Connected to Pune (250 km), Hyderabad (300 km), Mumbai (400 km), and Bengaluru (380 km)[cite: 52, 55, 56, 57, 58].

By Rail [cite: 59]

Solapur Railway Station is a major junction connecting Mumbai, Pune, Hyderabad, Chennai, and Bengaluru[cite: 60].

By Air [cite: 62]

Small local airport with limited services. Nearest major airports are Pune and Hyderabad[cite: 63, 64, 65].

Local Transport

Move around using auto rickshaws, taxis, and city buses[cite: 68, 69, 70, 71].
